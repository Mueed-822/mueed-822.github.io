He Dianning (何殿宁) is an Associate Professor and Master's Supervisor at the School of Health Management, China Medical University. He received his B.S., M.S., and Ph.D. degrees from Northeastern University, China, and was a visiting scholar at the University of Chicago. His research focuses on intelligent medical image analysis and MRI technology, with over 50 published papers including 10+ SCI-indexed first/corresponding author papers. He has presented at top international conferences including RSNA 2016, 2017, and 2024, and holds multiple national invention patents.
